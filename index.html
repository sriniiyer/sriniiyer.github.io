Srinivasan Iyer

Hi, I'm Srini and I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Washington, Seattle. My advisors are Luke Zettlemoyer and Alvin Cheung. I am interested in Natural Language Processing. My main interests lie in joint modeling of natural language and source code. I am excited about using natural language to specify computer programs by learning from vast amount of web data, and in particular, I am interested in automatically converting utterances to SQL queries for arbitrary database schemas.

Before this, I worked at Facebook in the Ads Ranking and Optimization team. We used machine learning to ensure that users *love* the ads that they see on fb.

I got my Masters in CS from Stanford University where I worked on BioInformatics and Theoretical CS.

I like summiting peaks, traveling to national parks, and photography.
